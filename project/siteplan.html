RC Thunder - Website Planning Document

Site Name

RC Thunder: The Ultimate RC Car Enthusiast Hub

This name immediately conveys the subject matter (RC cars) and aims to excite the target audience with words like "Thunder" and "Ultimate," suggesting speed, power, and comprehensive resources. It clearly positions the site as a central hub for the hobby.

Optional domain availability: rcthunderhub.com (Hypothetical)

Site Purpose

The "RC Thunder" website will serve as a comprehensive informational and community hub for RC car enthusiasts, ranging from beginners seeking introductory knowledge to experienced hobbyists looking for detailed model reviews, DIY tips, and event information. It aims to foster a vibrant community and be the go-to resource for everything related to remote-controlled cars.

Scenarios

  • "As a beginner, where can I find a clear explanation of different RC car types and what's recommended for a starting budget?"
  • "I'm looking to upgrade my existing off-road RC car; where can I find detailed modification tutorials or product reviews?"
  • "Is there a calendar where I can find upcoming RC car races or meetups in my region?"

Color Schema

The carefully selected site colors are chosen to reflect the excitement, speed, and modern aspect of RC cars, while ensuring readability:

  • Primary Color (Headings, Main Accents): #FFD700 (Gold) - Represents premium quality, speed, and excitement.
  • Secondary Color (Backgrounds, Content Blocks): #2C3E50 (Dark Slate Gray) - Provides a strong, stable contrast to the gold, representing technology and power.
  • Accent Color (Links, Buttons, Dynamic Elements): #3498DB (Peter River - Bright Blue) - A vibrant color for call-to-action elements, symbolizing energy and progression.
  • Text Color: #ECF0F1 (Clouds - Light Gray) - Ensures high readability against darker backgrounds.

Typography

The chosen fonts aim to combine a modern, slightly futuristic feel with excellent readability for content:

  • Headings (h1, h2, etc.): 'Orbitron', sans-serif - A geometric sans-serif typeface that conveys a high-tech, dynamic, and forward-looking feel, suitable for the "Thunder" aspect.
  • Body Text (p, li, etc.): 'Roboto', sans-serif - A highly versatile and legible sans-serif font, ensuring comfortable reading for longer passages of information and tutorials.
